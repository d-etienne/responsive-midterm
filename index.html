POLICIES / FAQ

SHOP GUIDELINES:

  • Facemask Worn At All Times

HOW TO BOOK AN APPOINTMENT:

Head to our contact page and fill out our book an appointment form.

WHAT AGE DO YOU NEED TO BE TO GET A TATTOO?

18 without a parent's consent. 15-17 with a parent's consent. You would both need valid picture ID's. If you have different last names, then you need your child's birth certificate in addition to the valid picture ID's.

WHAT IS YOUR CANCEL / RESCHEDULE / LATE / NO SHOW POLICY?

Deposits are non-refundable. We have a 72 hour rescheduling policy. If you give us 72 hours notice we can reschedule your appointment for you to a different date and your deposit will transfer to the new appointment. We do understand that life comes up, but do also understand that this is our artists lives and they have dedicated this space of time to you and not other people.

Any cancellation or rescheduling after the 72 hour notice deadline will automatically forfeit your deposit and you will need to put down a new deposit to make a new appointment. Arriving more than 15 minutes late for your appointment will be considered a no show and you will forfeit your deposit and your appointment and will need to put down a new deposit to make a new appointment. Not showing up for your appointment is considered a "No Show" and your deposit is forfeited and all future appointments that were held by that deposit will be cancelled.
